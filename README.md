# Sales Performance & Business Insights Dashboard

## Objective
Analyze sales data to understand trends, identify underperforming regions and products, and uncover opportunities to improve revenue and profitability.

## Tools Used
- Excel: Data cleaning and preparation
- Tableau: Interactive dashboards and visualization

## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Month-on-Month Sales Growth
- Year-on-Year Sales Growth

## Key Insights
- Sales show consistent year-over-year growth with seasonal fluctuations.
- Month-on-Month growth is volatile, indicating short-term demand variability.
- Western Europe is the highest revenue-generating region.
- Technology category contributes the highest profit.
- Consumer segment accounts for the majority of sales.
- Profit margin indicates scope for cost optimization.
